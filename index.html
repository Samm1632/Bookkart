Shyam Keshariya is a 21 year old, residing in Gujarat. He is a versatile writer, a poet and a lyricist. He is a third year student, studying IT engineering, from Marwadi University. He completed his schooling from Saint Thomas, Atmiya, Utkarsh and Tapasvi School. Other than writing, his passion is photography and music.

He published his first book on 7th July, 2020 which was "An Elixir of Love" and currently his working on his next book called "Angel and her Lover" which is soon going to be launched lately this year.

His journey started with writing lyrics which inspired him to write quotes, conversations, poems and letters. His work got appreciated by many people at many open mics and his social media that's when he thought to publish his own book!
